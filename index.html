Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1892

Founded in Paris by master jeweler Henri Dubois, Éclat has been creating extraordinary jewelry for over a century. Our pieces are worn by royalty, celebrities, and discerning clients who appreciate the finest things in life.

Each Éclat creation begins with ethically sourced gemstones and precious metals, transformed by our artisans into wearable works of art. We maintain the highest standards of quality, ensuring every piece meets our exacting criteria.
