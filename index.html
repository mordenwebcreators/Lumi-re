Share the Whole Universe

Founded in 2010, Lumière Jewelry was born from a passion for craftsmanship and timeless elegance. Our founder, Isabelle Laurent, envisioned creating pieces that would tell stories and become family heirlooms.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by our master jewelers using only the finest materials. We source ethical diamonds and gemstones, and our gold is 100% recycled.

At Lumière, we believe jewelry should be more than an accessory - it should be a reflection of your inner light, a celebration of life's precious moments, and a connection to something greater.
